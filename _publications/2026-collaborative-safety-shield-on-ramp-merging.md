This preprint presents a collaborative safety shield framework designed for safe and efficient lane changes by Connected and Autonomous Vehicles (CAVs) in congested on-ramp merging scenarios. The proposed approach extends prior safety shielding methods to cooperative multi-vehicle settings, addressing the challenges of high traffic density and interactive behaviours among autonomous agents.
